Learning and Takeaways

6 February 2025

Topic Covered: E-Portfolio Making and Main Objective of the Class

In this session, we were introduced to the concept and importance of creating an e-portfolio. We learned that an e-portfolio is more than just a digital resume—it reflects our personal growth, skills, achievements, and career aspirations in one place.

Key Takeaways:

  • The main objective of the class is to help us understand ourselves better and prepare professionally for future opportunities.
  • Creating an e-portfolio will enhance our visibility, confidence, and career readiness.
  • This class laid the foundation for everything we’re building through self-reflection and presentation.

13 February 2025

Topic Covered: Personal Profiling & SWOC Analysis

We explored the importance of understanding ourselves through personal profiling and SWOC analysis.

"To grow and perform effectively in any field, you must first be aware of your strengths, recognize your weaknesses, and value your unique potential."

This realization emphasized that self-awareness is not just a personal asset but a professional necessity.

24 February 2025

Topic Covered: Presentation Skills

This session focused on building effective presentation skills. We discussed how communication is not just about speaking, but also about clarity, confidence, and body language.

Key Takeaways:

  • Be clear and organized in your thoughts.
  • Listen actively and attentively.
  • Speak confidently and with appropriate volume.
  • Maintain good posture and body language.

3 March 2025

Topic Covered: Presentation Skills

In this session, we focused on the personal aspect of presentation—how to present ourselves, not just content. The emphasis was on creating a lasting impression through appearance, expression, and confidence.

Key Learnings:

  • Dress appropriately and carry yourself with confidence.
  • Make eye contact and smile to build connection.
  • Speak with clarity and maintain a calm tone.
  • Be authentic and mindful of your body language.

7 April 2025

Topic Covered: Email Writing

We learned how to write professional emails that are clear, concise, and courteous. This session emphasized proper structure (subject, salutation, body, and closing), tone, and formatting.

Effective email writing plays a crucial role in professional communication and leaves a strong impression on the recipient.

21 April 2025

Topic Covered: Soft Skills and Their Importance

This session shed light on how soft skills like communication, teamwork, time management, and adaptability are just as important as technical knowledge.

“Technical skills may get you a job, but soft skills help you grow in your career.”

28 April 2025

Topic Covered: Group Discussion (Introduction)

We were introduced to the format and importance of group discussions in interviews and teamwork.

Takeaways:

  • How to start a GD confidently.
  • How to express your point without dominating.
  • The value of listening and responding logically.

5 May 2025

Topic Covered: Group Discussion Practice (In-Class Round)

In this practical GD round, I had the chance to apply what I learned previously. It helped boost my confidence and taught me to stay calm under pressure.

I learned the importance of staying on topic, supporting points with logic, and encouraging quieter members to speak—skills essential for leadership and collaboration.
