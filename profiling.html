Personal Profiling

Write your career objective.

Why do you want to be an engineer? Elaborate reasons.

Write about a failure of yours which you consider to share. What have you learned from it?

What are your strengths? Write one or two instances where you have demonstrated your strengths.

Write about your weaknesses. What are you doing to overcome your weaknesses?

What is the most difficult moment that you have faced in your life so far? What qualities helped you to overcome the moment?

Apart from academics, what else are you interested in?

Describe yourself as an individual in 5 lines.

  • I am a highly focused and disciplined learner.
  • I value consistency and hard work over shortcuts.
  • I’m spiritual and believe in inner peace for outer success.
  • I am emotionally strong yet humble in behavior.
  • I enjoy both technology and creativity equally.

What kinds of people do you enjoy working with?

What kinds of people you don’t want to work with? What would you do if they became your senior in your dream job?

In the past year, what have you been dissatisfied about in your performance?

Rate yourself out of 5 in verbal communication. What are you doing to improve your communication skills?

Who is your role model? What qualities of that person you would like to see in your personality and why?

Write a few lines about your friends. Do you think they help/may help you in achieving your goals? If yes, how? If no, why do you accompany them?

Write 3 leadership qualities. How many do you possess? Write an instance where you have applied those qualities.

So finally, tell us something more about yourself or introduce yourself.
